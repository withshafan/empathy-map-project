❤️ FEELS Internal

Emotions & deep struggles

  • 😣 Overwhelmed by work-life imbalance
  • 😔 Ashamed of her fitness decline
  • 😟 Anxious about health reports
  • 😤 Frustrated that nothing works for her schedule

⚡ The Barriers to Entry (Friction Points)

“Design for their worst day, not their best.”

1️⃣ Back-to-back meetings

No 30-min free slot. Even lunch is eaten at desk while typing. The calendar is a wall of red.

2️⃣ Commute exhaustion

1.5 hours travel each way. Leaves home at 7 AM, returns at 8 PM. Zero energy for any complex routine.

3️⃣ Mental fatigue = zero motivation

After 50+ Slack messages and 6 hours of Zoom, brain is fried. Can't think about workout plans or meal prep.

📌 Why existing fitness apps fail for her: They assume she has energy, time, and a gym nearby. She needs 7-minute desk exercises, habit stacking, and guilt‑free rest days.
🧩 Insight: Ayesha doesn't need a gym. She needs micro-workouts, habit stacking, and guilt‑free rest days.
Design for her worst day (exhausted, 10 PM) – not her best.
